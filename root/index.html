About

Image Analysis uses machine learning to predict how many likes and comments your picture will get. Getting a prediction is easy. First, if you're a new user, connect with your Instagram account. Once connected, you need to train your individualized machine learning model by clicking the "Train Model" button. This may take some time. Feel free to leave the page, run to the store, do some errends, etc. while training is in progress. Once trained, you'll be able to upload an image and get a prediction within minutes.
